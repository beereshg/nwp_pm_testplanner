Root Cause Summary

According to PMT spec, Telemetry Sampler Register Definition,

For One-shot , it is armed once, and hardware should clears the sample mode when the data is collected. If the sample mode is cleared before the data is collected the data collection may or may not be aborted.

But This HW mechanism was not correctly implemented in Anot happened on IMH and CBB pcode sampler watcher.
