Root Cause Summary

On a DMR AP X1 system, we are seeing a very low PC6 Residency of ~49%, this in turn is pushing OS Idle power ~30W over the target.

One of the reasons for such a low residency is due to D2D L1. When D2D interface entered L1 state as part of PC6, its residency is very low ~42%. This is limiting overall PC6 Residency.

When we try to disable D2D L1, that further reduces PC6 residency to ~27%. This is due to a known issue where CBB D2D Qchannel FSM keeps denying the Idle request.
