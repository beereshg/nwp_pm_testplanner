## Section A: NWP Disposition & Justification

**Disposition: Runnable_On_N-1**

When MR4 temperature crosses the memtrip threshold, the MC asserts `ip_temp_memtrip` → xxMEMTRIP GPIO → causes xxTHERMTRIP assertion (if `memtrip_to_thermtrip` enabled). `thermtrip_config_cfg.memtrip_to_memtrip` is enabled by default.

Tags: `DMR_PO`, `NGA_MAIN`, `plc.ti_gate.b0`, `plc.feature.p1`, `PMSS_NWP_READINESS_CHECK`.
