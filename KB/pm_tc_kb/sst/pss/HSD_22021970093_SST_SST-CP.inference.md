## Refined Intent
SST-TF: Verify turbo ratio is clipped to LP_CLIP_RATIO when all cores are assigned to Low Priority (CLOS2/CLOS3). Enable SST-TF, assign all cores to CLOS2 via SST_CLOS_ASSOC registers, verify XEON_CORE module_max_ratio matches LP clipping ratio.

## Refined Test Steps
Pre-Conditions:
  - SST-TF enabled in BIOS
  - All C-states disabled

Step 1 — Enable SST-TF:
  Write 1 to SST.SST_PP_CONTROL.FEATURE_STATE[1].

Step 2 — Assign all cores to CLOS2 (LP):
  Write 2 to all fields in SST.SST_CLOS_ASSOC_<0, 1, 2, 3>.

Step 3 — Determine bucket number:
  Read SST.SST_TF_INFO_1.

Step 4 — Obtain LP clipping ratio:
  Read SST.SST_TF_INFO_0.LP_CLIP_RATIO_<0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5>.

Step 5 — Obtain SST-PP SSE/CDYN0 TRL ratio:
  Read SST.SST_PP_INFO_4.

Step 6 — Verify per-core ratio:
  For each active core, check XEON_CORE module_max_ratio matches LP_CLIP_RATIO (not exceeding it).

Step 7 — Repeat with CLOS3:
  Assign all cores to CLOS3, repeat Steps 3-6.

Pass/Fail Criteria:
  PASS: All cores clipped to LP_CLIP_RATIO, none exceeding it
  FAIL: Any core exceeding LP ratio
