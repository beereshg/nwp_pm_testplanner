## Refined Intent
Verify PCT can be dynamically enabled/disabled via BIOS. When enabled, HP cores follow HP PCT ratio and LP cores follow LP PCT ratio. When disabled, all cores clip to P0n.

## Refined Test Steps
Pre-Conditions:
  - PCT supported (fuse enabled)
  - All C-states disabled

Step 1 — Enable PCT, verify HP/LP ratios:
  After booting with HP core selection:
  Set HWP MSR 0x774 to request max frequency on all cores.
  Verify HP cores follow HP PCT ratio.
  Verify LP cores follow LP PCT ratio.

Step 2 — Disable PCT via BIOS and reboot:
  Verify all cores clip to P0n (unified ratio, no HP/LP differentiation).

Step 3 — Enable PCT via BIOS and reboot:
  Verify HP/LP differentiation returns (same as Step 1).

Pass/Fail Criteria:
  PASS: Dynamic enable shows HP/LP split, disable shows unified P0n
  FAIL: HP/LP split persists after disable, or no split after enable
